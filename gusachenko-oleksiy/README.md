<h1>Soundcloud</h1>
<a href="https://soundcloud.com/"><img src="https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/ru/thumb/9/92/SoundCloud_logo.svg/1200px-SoundCloud_logo.svg.png" width="100" height="50"></a>
<p>Welcome to SoundCloud.com! This user guide will provide you with an overview of the features and functionalities available on SoundCloud's website, helping you navigate and make the most out of your experience.</p>
<h2>Signing Up and Logging In:</h2>
<li>To get started, visit SoundCloud.com on your web browser.</li>
<li>Click on the <a href="https://soundcloud.com/signin/forgot">"Create account"</a> button to sign up or use your existing Google or Facebook account to log in.</li>
<li>Fill in the required information, including your username, password, and email address, to create an account.</li>
<li>After signing up or logging in, you'll have access to additional features and be able to personalize your SoundCloud experience.</li>
<h2>Exploring Music:</h2>
<li>SoundCloud is a platform for music creators and enthusiasts. You can explore a vast collection of music tracks, podcasts, and audio content shared by artists worldwide.</li>
<li>Use the search bar at the top to find specific artists, tracks, or genres.<img src="https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachments/1111639697421258815/1112438622780670053/image.png" width="800" height="450">
<p>You can also use hashtags to discover trending content.</p></li>
<li>Browse through the <a href="https://soundcloud.com/discover">Explore section</a> to discover popular tracks, charts, and recommended content based on your listening preferences.</li>
<h2>Listening to Music:</h2>
<li>When you find a track or playlist you want to listen to, simply click on its title or thumbnail to open the player.</li>
<img src="https://github.com/Gusachenko-Alex/kpi-tech-writer-course/assets/59765750/9e8538b5-3023-4867-944d-a44f51c62069" width="800" height="200">
<li>The player will appear at the bottom of the screen, allowing you to control playback. You can pause, play, skip tracks, adjust the volume, and repeat or shuffle the playlist.</li>
<img src="https://github.com/Gusachenko-Alex/kpi-tech-writer-course/assets/59765750/7fce5d86-7847-40c0-968c-a5e74405c691" width="800">
<li>You can also like tracks, repost them to your profile, or add them to your personal playlists for easy access.</li>

<h2>Interacting with Artists and the Community:</h2>
<li>SoundCloud encourages engagement between listeners and artists. You can leave comments on tracks, show appreciation, or provide feedback.</li>

<li>Follow your favorite artists to stay updated with their latest releases. Their new tracks will appear in your stream.</li>

<h2>Uploading and Sharing Your Own Music:</h2>
<li>SoundCloud allows you to upload your own music and share it with the community.</li>
<li>Click on the <a href="https://soundcloud.com/upload">"Upload"</a> button to upload your tracks or recordings. You can add metadata such as titles, descriptions, and artwork to enhance the presentation.</li>
<li>Set your tracks as public, private, or unlisted depending on your preference. Public tracks are visible to everyone, while private tracks require a direct link to access.</li>
<li>Share your tracks on social media or embed them on websites using the provided share options.</li>
<h2>Managing Your Profile:</h2>
<li>Customize your profile by adding a profile picture, cover image, and description. This helps others learn more about you and your musical interests.</li>
<img src="https://github.com/Gusachenko-Alex/kpi-tech-writer-course/assets/59765750/d5daffc4-5e70-45f8-88c5-f88bfdca84d7" width="800" height="550">
<li>Manage your tracks, playlists, and followers through your profile. You can organize your music into playlists and make them accessible to others.</li>
<img src="https://github.com/Gusachenko-Alex/kpi-tech-writer-course/assets/59765750/e110d139-43b7-4083-8825-d6269a7439aa">

<h2>SoundCloud Go and SoundCloud Go+:</h2>
<li>SoundCloud offers premium subscription plans, SoundCloud Go and <a href="https://checkout.soundcloud.com/artist?ref=t148">SoundCloud Go+</a>, which provide additional benefits such as ad-free listening, offline playback, and access to exclusive content. These plans require a separate subscription.</li>
<h2>Mobile Apps:</h2>
<li>SoundCloud offers mobile apps for iOS and Android devices. You can <a href="https://apps.apple.com/us/app/soundcloud-discover-new-music/id336353151">download</a> the app from the respective app stores to access SoundCloud on the go.</li>
<h3>Remember to respect the copyrights of artists and content creators when using SoundCloud. Enjoy exploring, discovering new music, and connecting with fellow music enthusiasts!</h3>
<h4>For more detailed information and assistance, you can visit the <a href="https://help.soundcloud.com/hc/en-us/requests/new?ticket_form_id=581048"> SoundCloud Help Center</a> or reach out to their customer support team directly. Happy listening!</h4>
